Encebollado

Description

The encebollado is a typical dish of Ecuadorian cuisine. In particular, it is a dish from the coastal region.
This soup is one of the best fish soups in the world, according to the Taste Atlas ranking, and is one of the
favourite dishes of Ecuadorians. (For more information read this article).

Preparation

Preparation time: 1 to 2 hours
  1. In a large pot, add water, onion, tomato, pepper, crushed garlic, fresh ginger, salt, pepper, cumin and oregano.
    Mix well and wait for the broth to heat up.

  2. Add the dried chilli (previously hydrated in water) and the albacore just before the stock begins to boil.

  3. Add the bundles of coriander and parsley to flavour the preparation.

  4. Cut the cassava into large pieces and cook it in plenty of water.

  5. Remove the fish from the broth and let it cool.

  6. Remove the bundles of coriander and parsley, as well as the ginger from the stock.

  7. Blend the remaining vegetables from the stock, and add them back to the pot through a sie

  8. Add the cassava cooking water to the stock.

  9. Cut the cooked cassava into smaller pieces and have some chopped and rinsed onion on hand.

  10. Fractionate the fish and add to the broth along with the cassava.

  11. Serve adding yucca, fish, onion and fresh coriander on a plate and cover with the broth.

  12. Serve with lemon, chifles and/or bread.
